About Hominess

A Vision for Hominess


Hominess is not just a restaurant; it's an experience. Nestled in a charming corner of Tirana, this culinary gem offers a unique twist on dining with its exclusive igloo rooms. Stepping into Hominess is like entering a winter wonderland, where the ambiance is warm, the food is delightful, and the atmosphere is nothing short of enchanting...


The highlight of Hominess is undoubtedly its igloo rooms. These private dining spaces offer a cocoon-like escape, providing an intimate setting for couples, families, or groups of friends. The igloos are thoughtfully designed with plush seating, ambient lighting, and even a touch of winter-themed decor to enhance the overall experience.


Each igloo is equipped with a heating system to keep guests comfortably warm, creating a cozy haven even on the coldest nights. The semi-transparent walls allow diners to enjoy the beauty of the surroundings while maintaining a sense of privacy.

"Hominess: Where the art of dining meets the science of comfort. In our igloo retreats, find inspiration in every flavor and joy in every shared moment."

A Legacy in the making


As you approach Hominess, you're greeted by a façade that seamlessly blends modern aesthetics with a touch of rustic charm. The exterior boasts a series of igloo-inspired structures, each housing a private dining room. Lit from within, these igloos cast a soft, inviting glow that beckons guests to step inside and experience a dining atmosphere like no other.


Hominess takes pride in offering a diverse menu that caters to various culinary preferences. Whether you're a fan of hearty comfort food or you prefer more refined dishes, the menu at Hominess has something for everyone.


The staff at Hominess is dedicated to providing top-notch service, ensuring that every guest feels pampered and attended to. From the moment you step into your private igloo to the last bite of dessert, the team at Hominess strives to create a memorable and enjoyable dining experience.


"Hominess: A sanctuary where the ordinary becomes extraordinary. Our igloo rooms are not just spaces; they are canvases for the art of creating unforgettable moments."

Special Events


Hominess is not just a restaurant; it's a venue for special occasions. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or simply want a unique dining experience, the igloo rooms can be reserved for private events, making your celebration truly one-of-a-kind.


In summary, Hominess is more than just a restaurant with igloo rooms; it's a destination that invites guests to escape into a world of warmth, culinary delights, and unparalleled hospitality.
