Welcome to

Hominess

Good Time, Great Taste



A one-of-a-kind restaurant that combines culinary excellence with a unique dining experience. Nestled in a quiet and private side of a bustling city, Hominess stands out with its innovative design featuring igloo rooms, creating an enchanting and cozy atmosphere for guests.


What truly sets Hominess apart are its exclusive igloo rooms. These private dining spaces are each crafted to resemble a traditional igloo, complete with transparent walls that provide a breathtaking view of the surrounding cityscape. Inside, guests are welcomed by plush seating, soft blankets, and ambient lighting, creating an intimate and cozy ambiance.



"At Hominess, we believe that every meal is a journey and every guest is a fellow traveler. Join us in our igloo haven, where stories unfold, and friendships are kindled over plates of culinary magic."
